About,
the maker
( Studio portrait ) FIG. 02 — ATELIER
"I work at the intersection of visual language and cultural intuition — making brands that feel inevitable."

I'm a UX/UI and communication designer with experience in agency work, from events to digital projects. I work at the intersection of branding, interfaces, and visual communications: I can build systems and concepts, as well as quickly complete practical tasks manually.

I graduated from the Russian Presidential Academy of National Economy and Public Administration (RANEPA) with a degree in Graphic Design and additionally completed training in UX/UI. During my time at agencies, I led projects for major brands.
