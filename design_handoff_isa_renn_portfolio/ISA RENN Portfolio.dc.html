About,

the maker

( Studio portrait ) FIG. 02 — ATELIER

I direct image and identity for fashion houses, magazines and cultural institutions — treating every project as a curated body of work rather than a deliverable.

Trained between Paris and Milan, my practice moves fluidly across campaign, print and the moving image. The work is restrained, typographic and unmistakably editorial.

I build with collaborators — stylists, photographers, writers — and lead from concept through to the final printed spread, holding a single uncompromising point of view.
